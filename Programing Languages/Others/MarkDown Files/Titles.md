You can create titles by placing one or more `#` symbols in front of your text. The number of `#` symbols determines the heading level—just like `<h1>`, `<h2>`, `<h3>` in HTML.

```
# Title (H1)
## Title (H2)
### Title (H3)
#### Title (H4)
##### Title (H5)
###### Title (H6)
```

Result:
# Title (H1)
## Title (H2)
### Title (H3)
#### Title (H4)
##### Title (H5)
###### Title (H6)
