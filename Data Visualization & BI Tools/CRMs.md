### What is a CRM?

Is a system that helps companies manage how they interact with their customers—both current and potential.

- The customer is at the center of the business. All processes are organized to improve the relationship with them.
    
- It’s not just software—it’s also a business strategy to build customer loyalty.
    
- CRMs help create long-lasting and satisfying relationships with customers.
    
- Several departments are involved: sales, marketing, and customer service (those with the most contact with the client).


A CRM helps companies better understand their customers, organize information, and improve long-term relationships.

A CRM can be part of an ERP (as an integrated module) or work as a separate tool connected to the ERP.
