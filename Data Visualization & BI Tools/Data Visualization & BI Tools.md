This section explores three essential categories of digital tools that support modern business operations. Each serves a distinct purpose in helping organizations manage resources, customer relationships, and strategic decisions:

### [[ERPs]]
**Enterprise Resource Planning**: Software that integrates key business functions—like finance, logistics, and HR—to streamline operations and improve decision-making.

### [[CRMs]]
**Customer Relationship Management**: Tools focused on managing customer interactions, sales pipelines, and marketing efforts.

### [[BL Tools]]
**Business Intelligence Tools**: Platforms that analyze data and generate insights to guide strategic decision-making.
